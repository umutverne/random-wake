# Privacy Policy for RandomWake

**Last Updated: January 8, 2025**

## Introduction

RandomWake ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally

RandomWake stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Alarm Settings**: Time ranges, repeat days, task types, sound preferences, and labels you configure
- **Statistics**: Wake-up success rates, snooze counts, and streak data
- **App Preferences**: Theme (dark/light), language, and other settings

**This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.**

### Information Collected by Third Parties

#### Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

- Device identifiers
- IP address
- General location (country/region)
- App usage data for ad personalization

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

## Permissions We Request

RandomWake requires the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| **Notifications** | To alert you when your alarm rings |
| **Vibration** | To vibrate your device during alarms |
| **Schedule Exact Alarm** | To ensure alarms ring at the correct time |
| **Boot Completed** | To restore alarms after device restart |
| **Full Screen Intent** | To display alarm screen over lock screen |

## Data Security

All your alarm and preference data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. We do not have access to this data.

## Children's Privacy

RandomWake is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
